About Us


JOEL COWDEN, CPA
OWNER

Joel has more than 13 years of accounting experience working for companies of all shapes and sizes and across many industries such as construction, healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, and more.

After graduating with a Master of Accountancy degree from Brigham Young University, Joel spent 6 years with Ernst & Young where he was an audit manager and worked with more than 20 companies in over 10 industries. Over the next 7 years, Joel honed his accounting skills in the corporate world where he held senior management positions at i-transfer, Xerox, and Arrow Electronics.

Joel is passionate about helping business leaders lighten their load of running a successful business. He takes the time to listen to and understand his customers’ needs and partners with them to find lasting solutions. In addition, his experience with advanced accounting systems allows him to help businesses of all sizes maximize their own accounting systems and make them more efficient. Joel has active CPA licenses in the states of Colorado and California.

Joel and his wife have 4 daughters. Joel enjoys spending time with his family, especially in the beautiful Colorado outdoors.
